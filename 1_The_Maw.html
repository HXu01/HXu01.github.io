The Maw

Tool: Clip Studio Paint

This is my digital painting created using CSP. For this painting, I wanted to put a dark romanticism spin on top of traditional environment painting. As for the subject, I wanted to convey horror through nothing more than a slight abstraction to nature.

"The dark ocean opens its dark maw, violently and hungerliy swallowing all light from the heavens."
