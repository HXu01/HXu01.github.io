The Trash Flower

2023 Fall

Tool: Adobe Photoshop

This is a combination of real life sculpture and photo manipulation. The image consists of two separate sculptures merged through masking techniques. The images underwent editing in Photoshop, focusing on texture, composition, and color enhancement. This piece is particularly unique as I rarely venture into sculpture outside of traditional mediums. Both sculptures were crafted entirely from garbage found in my room, primarily consisting of tin foil.

"A flower of trash, beauty blooming from the garbage pile."
