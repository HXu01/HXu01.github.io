Kool-aid

2023 Fall

Tool: Adobe Animate, Clip Studio Paint

This is an animation I created using CSP and Adobe Animate, incorporating a 12-second audio clip from the "2 Bears 1 Cave" podcast. The animation aimed to emphasize facial expressions. Due to time constraints, there's a lack of polish, but overall, I believe it yielded decent results considering my limited knowledge in 2D animation and the time available

"You drinkin a 64 ounce Kool-aid... I drink 2 everyday"
